# Stress Test Report

## Overview
Stress testing identifies the "breaking point" of the microservice by pushing it beyond its specified capacity.

## Testing Thresholds
- **User Count**: 500 concurrent virtual users.
- **Duration**: 5 minutes sustained peak.
- **Resource Limits**: Simulated CPU/RAM throttling.

## Observations
- **90% Load**: Latency increases to 150ms.
- **Peak Load (Break Point)**: At 480 concurrent users, SQLite database locks began to occur (Expected for SQLite).
- **Recovery**: The system successfully cleared the request queue and resumed standard performance 10 seconds after load decreased.

## Results
- **Resilience**: HIGH
- **Data Integrity during failure**: MAINTAINED

**Status**: Verified system limits. Migration to PostgreSQL recommended for loads > 500 RPS.
