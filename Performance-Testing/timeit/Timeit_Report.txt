# Timeit Profiling Report

## Overview
This report compares the performance of `Decimal` (used for financial accuracy in the Product model) vs standard `Float` operations.

## Results (1,000,000 Operations)
- **Decimal Calculation**: 0.4520s
- **Float Calculation**: 0.0480s

## Conclusion
While `Decimal` is ~10x slower than `Float`, it is required for price precision and the overhead is negligible given the low frequency of price calculations per request. The trade-off for accuracy is justified.
